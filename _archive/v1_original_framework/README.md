## V1 Framework Structure

**6 Competency Buckets:**
1. Functional Excellence (Test Design, System Understanding, Bug Analysis)
2. Problem Solving (Root Cause, Risk Assessment, Prevention)
3. Impact & Ownership (PIC Behavior, E2E Accountability)
4. Communication (Dev/PM Alignment, Stakeholder Trust)
5. Process & Strategy (QA Improvement, Standards)
6. Culture & Values (Professionalism, Reliability, Humility)

**Dreyfus Levels:** 1-5 (Novice to Expert)

## Why we evolved to V2

- Simplify for public audience (Indonesia QA community)
- Align with ISTQB AI/GenAI certifications
- Focus on "problem at each level" not just skills
- Make AI integration explicit
- Reduce from 15 sub-competencies to 4 dimensions
